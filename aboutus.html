Welcome to Helping Hands, where kindness meets action. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of those in need.

The 'Helping Hand to Poor People' is a platform for the donors who want to donate their unused cloths, foods and stationary to the poor or low-income people who need those cloths, food and stationary.

A food bank is a non-profit, charitable organization that distributes food to those who have difficulty purchasing enough to avoid hunger, usually through intermediaries like food pantries and soup kitchens. Some food banks distribute food directly with their food pantries

Our mission is to empower communities, provide assistance to the underprivileged, and foster hope through our various programs and initiatives.

At Helping Hands, we believe in transparency, accountability, and the transformative power of collective generosity. Together, we can create a brighter, more compassionate world.

Join us in our journey to spread kindness and make a difference. Together, we can achieve greatness.
